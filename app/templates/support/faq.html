Frequently Asked Questions

SecureShare is a secure file sharing platform that allows you to upload, encrypt, and share files safely. All files are encrypted with military-grade AES-256 encryption, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

Very secure! We use AES-256 encryption (the same standard used by banks and military) to encrypt all files. Each file has a unique encryption key, and all access is logged for security auditing.

We support most common file types including:
  • Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PPT, PPTX
  • Images: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF
  • Archives: ZIP, RAR, 7Z
  • Text: TXT
Maximum file size is 16MB.

Share links are unique, cryptographically secure URLs that allow anyone to download your file. The links contain no identifying information about you or the file name, ensuring privacy. You can generate and copy share links from your "My Files" page.

For your security, passwords must meet these requirements:
  • At least 8 characters long
  • At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • At least one number (0-9)
  • At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

For security, login sessions automatically expire after 30 minutes of inactivity. You'll receive a warning 5 minutes before expiration, giving you time to save your work or extend your session.

If you're experiencing upload issues, try these steps:
  1. Check that your file is under 16MB
  2. Verify the file type is supported
  3. Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  4. Try refreshing the page and uploading again
  5. If problems persist, contact our support team

If you can't find the answer to your question here, please visit our Contact Support page to send us a message. We typically respond within 24 hours.
